You never have to study alone again.

Don't know anyone in your class? Missed a TA session or office hours? Having difficulty finding other people to study with? Now you can find and form study groups at the click of a button with Study Buddy, the app that connects you with groups of students on campus who share the same goal: to collaborate on homework assignments and help each other study for exams.

Created by Aaron Plave, Aaron Rosen, Hora, Denise Francisco and Lili Borland at WesHack 2014
